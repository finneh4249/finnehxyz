# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Technical recruiters, engineering leadership (CTOs/VPs), and the AI engineering community looking for systems-level expertise.

## Product Purpose
A high-stakes professional portfolio and technical logbook that demonstrates systems architecture expertise and AI engineering proficiency through an uncompromising industrial aesthetic. It serves as a proof-of-work instrument for shipping reliable, production-grade systems.

## Brand Personality
Aggressive expertise, infrastructural weight, terminal precision. It should feel like a high-stakes diagnostic tool (aviation flight deck, rail signaling) rather than a generic developer portfolio.

## Anti-references
"SaaS-cream" pastels, rounded corners, generic minimalist portfolios, AI-generated "glassmorphism" templates, and anything that feels "safe" or "boring."

## Design Principles
- **Practice what you preach**: The interface should be as dense and technical as the systems it describes.
- **Expert confidence**: Use unapologetic scale and high-visibility "Signal" colors to demand attention.
- **Infrastructural integrity**: 0px radius corners and hard offsets to simulate physical, solid objects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG AAA where possible, prioritizing high contrast and legibility for technical documentation.
